About ResQMeal

Connecting surplus to need, one meal at a time

ResQMeal is a real-time platform that reduces food waste by connecting restaurants with surplus food to NGOs and volunteers who deliver it to people in need.

Our Mission

Our mission is to eliminate food waste and hunger by creating a seamless, technology-driven bridge between food surplus and food scarcity.
